What is Kimetsu No Yaiba?

a popular Japanese manga and anime series created by Koyoharu Gotouge
Set in Taisho-era Japan, the story follows Tanjiro Kamado,
a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his sister,
Nezuko, is turned into a demon. Tanjiro seeks revenge against the demon responsible while also searching for a cure to restore Nezuko to human form.
